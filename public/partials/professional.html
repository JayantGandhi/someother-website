Jay Gandhi

Born and raised in NYC with family and friends spread around the globe, pursuing a focus in international relations came naturally to me. Combining that with my love for technology has led to many interesting challenges and opportunities. I love picking up new languages (both human and non-human) and doing my best to understand complex situations from outside of my cultural perspective. While generally optimistic about the value of new technology, I am a proponent of a healthy dose of skeptism and taking a step back in order to assess the larger picture. On any given project my go to question is "What makes this X different from any other?" in an attempt to cut to the heart of why it is worth doing. When I am not working on bringing tech and international relations closer together, I enjoy doing improv and making short films.
